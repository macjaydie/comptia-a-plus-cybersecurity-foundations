**## Structure to Follow**



**Each topic in this section is documented using the following structure:**



**1. \*\*Overview\*\***

**- Brief explanation of the concept**

**- Why it exists and where it is used**



**2. \*\*Core Concepts\*\***

**- Key components and terminology**

**- How the technology works at a basic level**



**3. \*\*Security Relevance\*\***

**- How this component impacts system security**

**- Common risks or weaknesses**



**4. \*\*Attack Examples (High-Level)\*\***

**- Realistic examples of how this area may be abused**

**- No exploitation details, conceptual only**



**5. \*\*Defensive Considerations\*\***

**- Basic security controls or best practices**

**- What a defender should monitor or protect**



**6. \*\*Blue Team Perspective\*\***

**- Why this knowledge matters for SOC or defensive roles**
