# Safety Procedures in IT



IT professionals must follow safety procedures to protect themselves, equipment, and the environment.



---



## Electrical Safety



Electricity can cause injury or damage equipment.



Best practices:

- Turn off and unplug devices before working on them

- Use proper grounding

- Avoid working with wet hands



---



## Electrostatic Discharge (ESD)



Static electricity can damage sensitive components.



Prevention methods:

- Wear an anti-static wrist strap

- Use anti-static mats

- Store components in anti-static bags



---



## Lifting Safety



Improper lifting can cause injuries.



Best practices:

- Bend at the knees, not the back

- Do not lift heavy equipment alone

- Use proper lifting tools when needed



---



## Fire Safety



Electrical equipment can pose fire risks.



Best practices:

- Do not overload power outlets

- Keep flammable materials away from equipment

- Know the location of fire extinguishers



---



## Proper Disposal



Electronic waste must be disposed of safely.



Examples:

- Recycle batteries properly

- Follow regulations for disposing of old hardware
