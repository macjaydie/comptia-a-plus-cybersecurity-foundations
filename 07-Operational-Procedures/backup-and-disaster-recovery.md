# Backup and Disaster Recovery



Backups and disaster recovery plans ensure data can be restored after hardware failure, cyberattacks, or natural disasters.



---



## Importance of Backups



Backups protect against:

- Hardware failure

- Accidental deletion

- Malware and ransomware

- Natural disasters



---



## Types of Backups



### Full Backup

Copies all data.



### Incremental Backup

Copies only data changed since the last backup.



### Differential Backup

Copies data changed since the last full backup.



---



## Backup Best Practices



- Perform regular backups

- Store backups offsite or in the cloud

- Test backups to ensure they work



---



## Disaster Recovery Planning



A disaster recovery plan outlines how to restore operations after major failures.



It includes:

- Recovery procedures

- Contact information

- Backup locations

- System restoration priorities



---



## Why This Matters



Without backups, data loss can be permanent. Disaster recovery planning reduces downtime and business impact.
