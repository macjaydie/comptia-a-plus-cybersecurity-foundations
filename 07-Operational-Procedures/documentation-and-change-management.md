# Documentation and Change Management



Proper documentation and controlled changes are essential in IT environments. They ensure systems remain stable and issues can be traced and resolved efficiently.



---



## Importance of Documentation



Documentation helps IT teams:

- Track system configurations

- Record troubleshooting steps

- Share knowledge with other technicians

- Reduce repeated mistakes



Examples of documentation:

- Network diagrams

- System configuration records

- Incident reports

- Knowledge base articles



---



## Change Management



Change management is the process of planning and controlling changes to systems.



Why it matters:

- Prevents unexpected outages

- Reduces risk

- Ensures stakeholders are informed



---



## Change Management Process



Typical steps include:

1. Submit a change request

2. Review risks and impact

3. Obtain approval

4. Implement the change

5. Test the system

6. Document the results



---



## Why This Matters in IT Support



Uncontrolled changes can cause system failures. Documentation and change control ensure stability and accountability.
